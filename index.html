Bio

Dawn Kafka, guitarist, lead singer

Age: 28

Dawn was born in the heart of Chernobyl in a lower class family, days before
the disastrous explosion. They quickly moved to St. Petersburg. Despite this,
she began work at 9 years old, repairing instruments at her father's shop to
help earn money for her family. By the time she was 12, she was building
guitars and drum sets from the ground up, and knew how to skillfully play 13
different instruments. At age 15, her family moved to the Americas to start a
better life and to receive medical treatment for Dawn not available in Russia.
Dawn continued her passion in music, and she became a metalworker at 19
years old to earn a steady income. There, she met the rest of her band, and
they found their similarities in struggles and music bonded them on a strong
level. Battling poverty and being an outsider most of her life because of her
illness, Dawn finds inspiration in the struggles she once endured. She
believes that every person has the opportunity to rise up, but knows they are
often wrongfully pushed aside. Through Red Dawn, her goal is to inspire the
forgotten to make a name for themselves and be remembered.
